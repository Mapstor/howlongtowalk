## Why This Site Exists

Most "how long to walk a mile" results give you a vague range and a wall of SEO filler. We built something better.

**Real data.** Every calculation is grounded in the Bohannon & Andrews (2011) meta-analysis — the largest study of normal walking speeds ever published. Not guesses. Not AI-generated fluff.

**Age-adjusted.** Walking speed changes with age. Our calculator accounts for this using decade-by-decade data from 23,111 participants.

**Every distance.** From half a mile to 100 miles. From a 5K to an ultramarathon. One site, one calculator, every answer.

## Quick Reference: Average Walking Speed

The average adult walks at **3.0 mph** (4.8 km/h), which equals a 20-minute mile.

This varies by age and gender. Men aged 30–59 average 3.20 mph. Women aged 40–49 peak at 3.11 mph. Adults over 80 average about 2.1 mph.
