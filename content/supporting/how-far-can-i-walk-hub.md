## Your Walkable Radius

Think of your walking distance as a circle around your starting point.

At a moderate 3.0 mph pace, everything within a **1.5-mile radius** is reachable in 30 minutes. Everything within **3 miles** is reachable in an hour.

Our map tool shows this radius around your location. You might be surprised how many destinations — coffee shops, parks, grocery stores, friends' homes — fall within your walking range.

**Try it:** Allow location access above, and the map will show your personal walkable radius at each pace level.

## Walking Distance by Age

How far you can walk in a set time depends on your natural pace. Based on Bohannon & Andrews (2011):

| Age Group | 30-Min Distance (Men) | 30-Min Distance (Women) | 1-Hour Distance (Men) | 1-Hour Distance (Women) |
|-----------|------------------------|--------------------------|------------------------|-----------------------------|
| 20–29 | 1.52 mi | 1.50 mi | 3.04 mi | 3.00 mi |
| 30–49 | 1.60 mi | 1.50–1.56 mi | 3.20 mi | 3.00–3.11 mi |
| 50–59 | 1.60 mi | 1.47 mi | 3.20 mi | 2.93 mi |
| 60–69 | 1.50 mi | 1.39 mi | 3.00 mi | 2.77 mi |
| 70–79 | 1.41 mi | 1.27 mi | 2.82 mi | 2.53 mi |
| 80+ | 1.09 mi | 1.05 mi | 2.17 mi | 2.10 mi |

Even at age 80+, you can cover over a mile in 30 minutes. Walking remains one of the most accessible forms of exercise at every age.
