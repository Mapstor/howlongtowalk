## Walking Pace Reference

All times are based on six standard pace levels:

| Pace | Speed | Min/Mile | Who Walks This Fast |
|------|-------|----------|---------------------|
| Leisurely | 2.0 mph (3.2 km/h) | 30:00 | Window shopping, strolling |
| Easy | 2.5 mph (4.0 km/h) | 24:00 | Relaxed walk, conversation easy |
| Moderate | 3.0 mph (4.8 km/h) | 20:00 | Most adults' natural pace |
| Brisk | 3.5 mph (5.6 km/h) | 17:08 | Walking with purpose, breathing harder |
| Fast | 4.0 mph (6.4 km/h) | 15:00 | Power walking |
| Very Fast | 4.5 mph (7.2 km/h) | 13:20 | Race walking, athletic |

The CDC defines moderate-intensity walking as 2.5–4.0 mph. The British Journal of Sports Medicine (2018) defines brisk walking as approximately 100 steps per minute.
